About

I am a post-doctoral researcher in applied mathematics and computer Science at LIRIS and Institut Camille Jordan, Université Lyon 1. Before that I did my PhD with Boris Thibert And Quentin Mérigot at LJK - Université Grenoble Alpes. My main research interest is numerical optimal transport.
